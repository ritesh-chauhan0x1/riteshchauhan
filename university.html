About KIIT

KIIT (Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology) is a renowned university located in Bhubaneswar, Odisha, India. Known for its world-class infrastructure, diverse academic programs, and vibrant campus life, KIIT is a hub for innovation and excellence in education.

  • NAAC A++ Accredited
  • Over 30,000 students from across the globe
  • Modern research labs and facilities
  • Strong industry connections and placement record
  • Active student clubs and cultural events
